Description

Our pasta sauce has been called "Best home cooking by a bachelor" and "Better than the can of sauce by itself". Complete with ground beef (fully cooked), garlic, veggies, and even one spice - Oregeno - the simplicity will blow your mind.

Ingredients

  • Garlic - 5 cloves
  • Olive oil - 5 tablespoons
  • Storebought sauce - 1 can / 500ml
  • Tomatoes - 3x beefsteak or 5 if roma/on the vine
  • Something green - e.g. a broccoli crown or a zuccinni
  • Onion - 1 biggun
  • Ground Beef - 1-2 pounds
