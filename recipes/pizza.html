Description

Our pasta sauce has been called "Best home cooking by a bachelor" and "Better than the can of sauce by itself". Complete with ground beef (fully cooked), garlic, veggies, and even one spice - Oregeno - the simplicity will blow your mind.

Ingredients

  • Garlic - 5 cloves
  • Olive oil - 5 tablespoons
  • Storebought sauce - 1 can / 500ml
  • Tomatoes - 3x beefsteak or 5 if roma/on the vine
  • Something green - e.g. a broccoli crown or a zuccinni
  • Onion - 1 biggun
  • Ground Beef - 1-2 pounds
  • Salt and Pepper

Steps

  1. Heat a drizzle of oil in a sauce pan and fully cook the ground beef.
  2. Drain the beef and set it aside in a bowl.
  3. Small-dice the garlic and onion.
  4. In a saucepan, heat the olive oil on medium-high heat.
  5. Medium dice the onion, tomatoes, and green vegetables.
  6. Add the garlic to the pan, stirring casually for 2 minutes until fragrant.
  7. heat the Olive oil on medium-high heat again.
  8. Add the onion, tomatoes, and green vegetables to the pan.
  9. Lightly shake salt on the vegetables to open their pores.
  10. Fry the veggies for 5-10 minutes until they're soft and becoming translucent.
  11. To the pan of veggies, add the can of storebought sauce.
  12. Reduce heat to medium.
  13. Add a full layer of oregeno across the sauce.
  14. Cook for 5-10 minutes stirring frequently.
  15. Add the cooked ground beef to the sauce.
  16. Reduce heat to a simmer, and let simmer for 20 minutes.

If the sauce is drying out in the last step, add a quarter cup of water to allow time to simmer.
